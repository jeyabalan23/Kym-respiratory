It was founded by Liang Wenfeng, the co-founder of the High-Flyer hedge fund. The company is known for its open-source AI technologies, aiming to create cost-effective models comparable to OpenAI's GPT-4. In January 2025, DeepSeek launched its DeepSeek-R1 model, offering similar performance to other leading AI models at a fraction of the training cost. Despite challenges related to chip export restrictions to China, the company has attracted significant attention in the AI industry for its innovative approach.
